YodieHook Features

Aimbot

An aimbot is a feature that assists players in aiming and targeting opponents automatically.

ESP (Extra Sensory Perception)

ESP provides additional information about the game environment, such as the location of other players.

FakeDuck

FakeDuck is a technique used to manipulate the character's hitbox position, making it harder for opponents to hit.

AutoPeek

AutoPeek is a feature that helps players peek corners and engage opponents with improved timing.

Anti Screen Grab

Anti Screen Grab prevents unauthorized users from taking screenshots of your gameplay.

Fake Lag

Fake Lag simulates network lag to confuse opponents and make your movements unpredictable.

Bunny Hop

Bunny Hop allows players to perform rapid consecutive jumps to gain speed and agility.

Free Cam

Free Cam enables a free-floating camera view for exploring the game environment from any angle.
